# Code of Conduct

## Expected behavior

- Give constructive, specific feedback on code and ideas.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experience levels.
- Accept responsibility for mistakes and learn from them.
- Focus on what benefits the project and its users.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, flamebait, or deliberately derailing discussions.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or reviews.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces: issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other channel associated with react-haptics.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may, at their discretion:

1. Edit or remove comments, commits, or contributions that violate this code.
2. Temporarily or permanently ban contributors who engage in unacceptable behavior.

Decisions are final and will not be relitigated in public channels.

## Reporting

Report violations by emailing the maintainer directly or using GitHub's built-in reporting tools. Reports will be reviewed promptly and kept confidential.
